C major scale technique fundamentals.

- Start at 60 BPM with quarter notes; increase by 4 BPM when clean.
- Keep wrists loose; aim for evenness across fingers.
- Practice hands separately, then together.

Focus: timing stability, articulation clarity, evenness.

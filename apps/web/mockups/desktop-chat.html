Good ear -- you're right that something shifts in the middle section. What I'm hearing is that the left hand voice starts to lose its independence around bars 15-20. The two voices converge rhythmically, and the left hand ends up shadowing the right instead of maintaining its own melodic line.


This is one of the trickiest spots in this invention. Bach wrote the left hand as a true equal voice here, but it's natural for it to get pulled into the right hand's rhythm. Let me highlight exactly where it happens:
